After administration of vaccine the patient stood up quickly and passed out. The patient fell down and came to rather quickly. After she came to we helped her to a chair and sat her down, advised her to put her head between her knees, and got her some water that she requested. After a few minutes she appeared rather pale and went to ask her if she was ok and she asked for a trash can because she felt like she was going to be sick. We gave her a garbage bag and she did get sick in the garbage bag. We kept an eye on her for another 15-20 minutes and she stated that she felt better and that she thought she was ok to leave. We offered her help to her car but she denied it saying she would be ok with she walked slowly. Her father was with her the entire time. While talking with them the father did state that she had incidents like that in the past where she stood up too fast and got light headed and passed out.
