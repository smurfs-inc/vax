I woke up in the middle of the night (4/2-4/3) approx. 14 hours after receiving Vaccine with noticeable ringing/hissing in ears. This had never happened prior.I did not think much of it at the time and went back to sleep. On Saturday had intermittent ringing/hissing. By Sunday it was constant all day with no breaks. Seemed to get slightly worse each day from Monday through Wednesday. Thursday( Today) was the first morning were I woke up and the ringing/hissing was slightly less terrible than the day prior. As of writing this I have seen my primary care physician an ENT and an Audiologist. PCP and ENT both noted no visible issues in ear. Audiologist reported no damage to hearing. All three agreed it was Tinnitus.
