The occurrence of additional non-serious events is detailed below. The patient's past medical history included Generalized anxiety disorder and Gastric bypass in June 2020. Concomitant products included DIAZEPAM, DULOXETINE HYDROCHLORIDE (CYMBALTA) and ALPRAZOLAM (XANAX) for an unknown indication. On 08-Mar-2021, the patient received first dose of mRNA-1273 (Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine) (Intramuscular) 1 dosage form. On 05-Apr-2021, received second dose of mRNA-1273 (Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine) (Intramuscular) dosage was changed to 1 dosage form. On 05-Apr-2021, the patient experienced SUICIDAL IDEATION (Suicidal idealization) (seriousness criterion medically significant), FEELING ABNORMAL (felt bad), SWOLLEN TONGUE (tongue swollen), MENTAL DISORDER (Mental health issues), PARAESTHESIA ORAL (bad tingling in mouth), ERYTHEMA (Face is red) and VERTIGO (mind is spinning). At the time of the report, SUICIDAL IDEATION (Suicidal idealization), FEELING ABNORMAL (felt bad), SWOLLEN TONGUE (tongue swollen), MENTAL DISORDER (Mental health issues), PARAESTHESIA ORAL (bad tingling in mouth), ERYTHEMA (Face is red) and VERTIGO (mind is spinning) had not resolved. Treatment included benadryl. Patient mentioned he went to urgent care, his covid test was negative, psych evaluation recommended help for side effects only. The patient received both scheduled doses of mRNA-1273 prior to the event; therefore, action taken with the drug in response to the event is not applicable.
